Germany Check List - A1 German 8-Week Plan (Goethe + telc)

Week 1
- Learn alphabet, pronunciation, numbers, greetings, introducing yourself.
- Goal: Build 120 core words.

Week 2
- Articles (der/die/das), present tense verbs, question words.
- Goal: Read simple notices and short forms.

Week 3
- Daily routine vocabulary, time, dates, family, shopping.
- Goal: Listen to slow A1 dialogues and catch key details.

Week 4
- Cases (basic nominative/accusative), modal verbs, sentence order.
- Goal: Write short messages and fill forms.

Week 5
- Exam-focused listening drills (Goethe/telc mock audio).
- Goal: Reach 65%+ in listening mock parts.

Week 6
- Speaking preparation: self-introduction, asking/answering questions, requests.
- Goal: 3-minute A1 speaking simulation daily.

Week 7
- Full mock tests: one Goethe and one telc timed simulation.
- Goal: 70%+ in each section.

Week 8
- Weak-area revision + 2 final timed mocks + exam-day strategy.
- Goal: Stable pass-level scores before booking exam.

Daily template (90 minutes)
- 20 min vocabulary review
- 20 min grammar drills
- 20 min listening
- 20 min speaking practice
- 10 min writing task

Exam-week checklist
- Passport and exam registration details
- 2 mock papers completed under time pressure
- Speaking partner rehearsal
- Sleep and timing aligned with exam slot

Note: Always verify current exam format and official requirements on Goethe/telc pages before exam day.
